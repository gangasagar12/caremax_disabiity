Our Story

Built on Compassion & Excellence

CareMax was founded with a single, clear purpose: to provide the highest standard of personalised disability support. We understand that every individual's journey is unique, which is why we place you at the center of everything we do.

Over the years, we have grown into a trusted NDIS provider, known for our unwavering commitment to quality care, our person-centred approach, and our deeply compassionate philosophy. Our support goes beyond basic assistance; we strive to empower our participants to live fulfilling, independent, and joyful lives.
