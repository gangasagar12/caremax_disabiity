Social & Community Participation

Supporting Meaningful Connections and Active Community Involvement

About This Service

At CareMax Disability, we believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to participate fully in their community, build meaningful relationships, and enjoy activities that bring purpose and fulfillment to their lives. Our Social & Community Participation service is designed to help participants engage confidently in social, recreational, educational, and community-based activities.

We provide personalized support that encourages independence, inclusion, and personal growth while helping participants achieve their NDIS goals and enjoy a more connected lifestyle.

Social & Community Participation supports participants in accessing and engaging with their local community, social networks, recreational activities, and personal interests.

Our experienced support workers assist participants in developing confidence, improving social skills, building friendships, and exploring opportunities that enhance their overall wellbeing and quality of life.

Whether you want to join community groups, attend events, pursue hobbies, or simply become more active in your community, CareMax Disability is here to support your journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of activities can I participate in?

Participants can engage in social outings, community events, recreational programs, hobby groups, educational activities, and much more based on their interests.

Can I choose the activities I want to attend?

Yes. We work closely with participants to identify activities that align with their goals, preferences, and interests.

Is this service covered by NDIS funding?

Many participants may access Social & Community Participation supports through their NDIS plan. Our team can help you understand your available funding options.

Can support workers accompany me during activities?

Absolutely. Our support workers can provide assistance before, during, and after community activities as required.
