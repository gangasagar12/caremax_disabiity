About Social & Community Participation

Staying connected to the community is vital for wellbeing. We support you to participate actively in social, civic, and recreational activities. From joining a local club or hobby group to attending community events, we’re here to help you build meaningful relationships and enjoy your community.

How We Help

Social Outings

Going to cafes, movies, or community events.

Hobbies & Interests

Support to join clubs, classes, or recreational groups.

Community Engagement

Volunteering or participating in local community programs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you help me find a new hobby?

Absolutely! We love helping participants explore new interests and find groups.

Is the cost of the activity covered?

The NDIS typically covers the support worker’s time, while you may need to cover your ticket or entry fee.
