Travel & Transport

Helping You Stay Connected, Independent, and Active

About This Service

At CareMax Disability, we believe that reliable transportation plays an important role in maintaining independence, accessing essential services, and participating in community life. Our Travel & Transport service is designed to support participants in travelling safely and confidently to their desired destinations.

Whether it's attending medical appointments, going to work or education, participating in community activities, or completing everyday errands, our dedicated support team is here to help you stay connected and engaged.

Our Travel & Transport support assists participants who may face challenges using public transport or travelling independently due to their disability.

We provide safe, reliable, and person-centred transport assistance tailored to individual needs and NDIS goals. Our aim is to help participants access opportunities, maintain social connections, and participate actively in their communities.

Support can be provided on a regular basis or as needed, depending on your circumstances and goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of transport support do you provide?

We assist with travel to medical appointments, community activities, education, employment, shopping, and other essential destinations.

Can I receive regular transport support?

Yes. Transport services can be arranged regularly or based on individual requirements.

Is travel support included in my NDIS plan?

Participants with relevant NDIS funding may be eligible for transport supports. Our team can help you understand your funding options.

Can support workers accompany me during appointments?

Yes. Depending on your support needs, our staff can assist you before, during, and after your journey.
