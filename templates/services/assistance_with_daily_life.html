Assistance with Daily Life

Empowering Independence Through Everyday Support

About This Service

At CareMax Disability, we understand that daily tasks can sometimes be challenging. Our Assistance with Daily Life service is designed to provide personalized support that helps participants maintain independence, dignity, and confidence in their everyday routines.

Our experienced and compassionate support workers work alongside participants to deliver assistance tailored to their individual goals, preferences, and lifestyle. Whether you need support for a few hours a week or ongoing daily assistance, we are committed to helping you live comfortably and independently.

Assistance with Daily Life supports participants with essential day-to-day activities that enable them to live safely and independently at home and within their community. We focus on providing practical support while encouraging skill development and greater independence wherever possible. Every support plan is person-centred, flexible, and aligned with your NDIS goals.

Our team works closely with participants, families, and support networks to ensure that the support provided meets individual needs and promotes overall wellbeing.

How We Can Help

Personal Care Support

  • Assistance with bathing and showering
  • Grooming and personal hygiene
  • Dressing and clothing selection
  • Toileting support
  • Mobility assistance

Daily Household Activities

  • Meal preparation and cooking
  • Laundry assistance
  • Light household cleaning
  • Organizing living spaces
  • Grocery shopping support

Medication Assistance

  • Medication reminders
  • Support following healthcare routines
  • Assistance with daily health management tasks

Daily Routine Support

  • Morning and evening routines
  • Appointment preparation
  • Community participation preparation
  • Daily planning and organisation

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can access Assistance with Daily Life?

Participants with NDIS funding for daily living support may be eligible for this service. Our team can help determine the most suitable support options for your plan.

Can support be provided at home?

Yes. Most Assistance with Daily Life services are delivered in the participant's home environment.

Can support plans be customised?

Absolutely. Every participant receives a personalised support plan tailored to their individual goals and needs.

Is support available on weekends?

Yes. Support availability can be discussed with our team based on your requirements.
