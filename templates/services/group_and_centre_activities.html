Group & Centre Activities

Building Connections, Confidence, and Community Through Shared Experiences

About This Service

At CareMax Disability, we believe that meaningful social connections and community engagement play an important role in overall wellbeing and personal development. Our Group & Centre Activities service provides participants with opportunities to learn new skills, build friendships, gain confidence, and enjoy a variety of engaging activities in a safe and supportive environment.

Through structured group programs and centre-based activities, participants can explore their interests, develop independence, and become active members of their community while working towards their NDIS goals.

Group & Centre Activities are designed to support participants in developing social skills, increasing community participation, and enjoying meaningful experiences alongside others.

Our programs encourage participants to engage in recreational, educational, and skill-building activities that promote personal growth, confidence, and inclusion. Activities are tailored to suit different interests, abilities, and goals, ensuring every participant feels supported and valued.

Whether you enjoy creative activities, group outings, educational workshops, or social events, CareMax Disability offers opportunities to connect, learn, and grow.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of activities are available?

We offer social, recreational, educational, creative, and skill-building activities that support participant interests and goals.

Can I choose which activities to join?

Yes. Participants are encouraged to select activities that align with their interests and personal development goals.

Are activities suitable for different support needs?

Absolutely. Our programs are designed to be inclusive and can be adapted to suit individual abilities and support requirements.

Is transportation available?

Transport support may be available depending on individual circumstances and NDIS funding arrangements.

Are Group & Centre Activities covered by NDIS?

Many participants can access these supports through their NDIS funding. Our team can help determine eligibility and available supports.
