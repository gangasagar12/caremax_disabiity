About Group & Centre Activities

Our group and centre-based activities offer a fantastic way to meet new people, learn new skills, and have fun in a supportive environment. We organize a variety of workshops, social gatherings, and developmental programs tailored to the interests and goals of our participants.

We run activities both at our dedicated centre and out in the community.
