Household Tasks

Supporting a Safe, Comfortable, and Well-Maintained Home Environment

About This Service

At CareMax Disability, we understand that managing household responsibilities can sometimes become challenging. Our Household Tasks service is designed to help participants maintain a clean, safe, and comfortable living environment while promoting independence and wellbeing.

Our dedicated support workers provide practical assistance tailored to each participant's individual needs, preferences, and goals. Whether you require occasional support or ongoing assistance, we are committed to helping you live confidently and comfortably in your home.

Household Tasks support assists participants with everyday domestic activities that may be difficult to manage independently due to disability, health conditions, or other support needs.

Our goal is not only to provide assistance but also to encourage skill development and independence wherever possible. We work collaboratively with participants to create a living environment that supports their health, safety, and quality of life.

How We Can Help

Home Cleaning

  • Vacuuming and mopping floors
  • Dusting and surface cleaning
  • Kitchen cleaning
  • Bathroom cleaning
  • General household tidying

Laundry Assistance

  • Washing clothes and linens
  • Drying and folding laundry
  • Ironing and wardrobe organization

Meal Preparation Support

  • Basic meal preparation
  • Assistance with cooking
  • Kitchen organization
  • Cleaning after meal preparation

Grocery Shopping Assistance

  • Preparing shopping lists
  • Grocery shopping support
  • Unpacking and organizing groceries

Household Organisation

  • Decluttering living spaces
  • Organizing household items
  • Maintaining a safe home environment

Frequently Asked Questions

What household tasks can CareMax assist with?

We can support a wide range of domestic activities including cleaning, laundry, meal preparation, grocery shopping, and household organization.

Can support be provided regularly?

Yes. Services can be scheduled daily, weekly, fortnightly, or based on individual needs.

Will the support be personalised?

Absolutely. Every support plan is tailored to meet the participant's unique circumstances and goals.

Is this service funded through the NDIS?

Participants with appropriate NDIS funding may be able to access Household Tasks support. Our team can assist you in understanding your available supports.
